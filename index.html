Iced Matcha Latte

Ice, Matcha Powder, Oatmilk and Ice--Shake it up and enjoy!

Matcha Cream Cheese

Whisk Matcha and Cream Cheese together and use as a spread on toast or bagels!

Matcha Chai Tea Latte

Whisk Matcha using whisker, mix chai tea and add steamed milk. Enjoy!

Matcha Fruit Smoothie

Blend Matcha with Oatmilk and your favorite fruits for a filling breakfast!

Hot Matchocolate

Mix hot chocolate with whisked matcha and steamed milk. Top with whip and enjoy this warm drink!
